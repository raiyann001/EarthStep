Our story

Habits are how the planet actually changes.

EarthStep started with a simple observation: people care about sustainability, but caring rarely survives the gap between intention and Tuesday morning. So we built a system that closes that gap, one small challenge at a time.

Origin

Started by three people tired of guilt-based apps

EarthStep was founded in 2023 by a product designer, an environmental scientist, and a game developer who met while volunteering on a coastal cleanup. They noticed the same pattern in every sustainability app they'd tried: heavy on statistics, light on anything that made you want to open it the next day.

The team spent a year prototyping the Virtual Earth mechanic — a way to make invisible, cumulative impact visible and rewarding in the moment, instead of buried in a year-end report.

2023 — Founded

EarthStep is founded after a coastal cleanup meetup in San Diego.

2024 — First 10,000 users

Public beta launches with the Daily Challenges and Earth Health systems.

2025 — Virtual Earth 2.0

The interactive 3D Earth ships, tying visual growth directly to verified actions.

2026 — 128,000+ community

EarthStep crosses 2.1 million completed challenges worldwide.

What we believe

Our mission & values

Small over perfect

A five-minute action completed today beats a perfect plan you never start. We design for momentum, not guilt.

Make impact visible

People stay consistent when they can see the result. The Virtual Earth exists to make invisible progress tangible.

Community accountability

Habits stick when they're shared. Every challenge, badge, and streak is built to be more fun with other people.
