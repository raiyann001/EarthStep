From download to habit

How EarthStep works.

Four steps stand between you and a Virtual Earth that reflects real, sustained change.

1. Create your profile

Sign up in under a minute and tell us what matters to you — waste, water, energy, or transport — so challenges match your life.

2. Get a daily challenge

Each morning, EarthStep serves one clear action sized to take 5–20 minutes, based on your level and preferences.

3. Complete & verify

Do the action in the real world, then log it with a quick photo or check-in. Verification keeps the leaderboard honest.

4. Watch your Earth grow

XP, badges, and Earth Health update instantly — and your Virtual Earth visibly regenerates a little more.

How actions turn into a healthier Earth

Challenge completed

You log a verified action — say, a zero-waste lunch.

XP awarded

The action's difficulty and impact determine how much XP you earn.

Earth Health updates

Your consistency score adjusts, influencing how vibrant your Earth looks.

Virtual Earth regenerates

Forests, oceans, and skies on your globe shift to reflect your new score.
