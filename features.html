Everything inside EarthStep

Built to make sustainability stick.

Eight systems work together to turn one daily action into a habit, and one habit into a visible, growing Virtual Earth.

Daily Challenges

Every morning, EarthStep serves a new, achievable eco-action tailored to your habits — reduce plastic, bike instead of drive, or unplug idle electronics. Challenges scale with your level.

Virtual Earth

An interactive 3D globe that visually evolves with your actions — forests fill in, water clears, and clouds thin as your consistency improves. It's the one place your habits become visible.

Rewards

Trade earned XP for real discounts from sustainability-focused partners — reusable water bottles, plant-based meal kits, secondhand fashion, and local transit credits.

XP System

Every verified challenge earns experience points. XP compounds into levels, unlocking harder (and higher-impact) challenge tiers as you progress.

Earth Health

A single score from 0–100 that reflects your recent consistency. It rises with daily actions and gently decays with inactivity — a built-in nudge, not a punishment.

Badges

Over 60 collectible badges reward streaks, categories, and milestones — from a 7-day starter streak to the rare "Zero Waste Hero" earned across 100 waste-reduction challenges.

User Profile

Your profile is a living timeline — level, streak history, badge case, and a visual snapshot of how your Virtual Earth has changed since day one.

Progress Tracking

Weekly and monthly summaries translate your challenges into real units — liters of water saved, kilograms of CO₂ reduced, and items diverted from landfill.

Signature feature

Your Virtual Earth grows with you.

Behind every ring and badge is one living centerpiece: a 3D Earth rendered from your own activity data. Consistent challenge completion regenerates forests and clears haze; missed days let them fade. It's not a leaderboard number — it's a planet you can watch respond to what you do.
