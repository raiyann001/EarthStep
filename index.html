Inside the app

Habits that grow into something you can see.

Every feature in EarthStep is built to turn small, repeatable actions into a Virtual Earth you can watch come alive.

Daily Challenges

Fresh, bite-sized eco actions delivered each morning — from a 5-minute litter pickup to a meat-free dinner.

Virtual Earth

Your habits feed a living 3D Earth — forests spread, oceans clear, and skies clean as you show up.

Rewards

Redeem XP for real partner discounts on reusable gear, plant-based meals, and local transit passes.

XP System

Every verified action earns experience points that level up your profile and unlock new challenge tiers.

Earth Health

A single score that reflects your consistency — the more you act, the healthier your planet becomes.

Badges

Unlock collectible badges for streaks, milestones, and categories like Water Guardian and Zero Waste Hero.

User Profile

A living record of your journey — level, streak, badges earned, and the story of your Virtual Earth.

Progress Tracking

Weekly and monthly insights show exactly how your habits translate into measurable environmental impact.
