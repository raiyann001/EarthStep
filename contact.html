Common questions

FAQ

Is EarthStep free to use?

Yes. The core app — challenges, XP, badges, and your Virtual Earth — is completely free. Optional partner rewards may involve third-party pricing.

How are challenges verified?

Most challenges use a quick photo check-in or GPS confirmation. Group challenges add peer confirmation from other participants.

What happens if I miss a day?

Your streak resets, and Earth Health decays gradually — but nothing is deleted. You can pick a new challenge and start climbing again immediately.

Can I use EarthStep with my school or company?

Yes — reach out via the form above and our community team can help set up a group or organization space.
